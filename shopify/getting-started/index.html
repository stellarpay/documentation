What is Stellar?

The Stellar network is a distributed blockchain based ledger and database that facilitates cross-asset transfers of value, including payments. The native digital asset of Stellar is called Lumens (XLM).

What is StellarPay?

StellarPay is payment gateway service which is capable to process and notify any asset payment on Stellar network.

Can set customized conversion rates of any type asset on network. It makes possible create a stabilised private network channel between your business and clients

Also StellarPay never stores your private key or account password. In dashboard all operations happening on your client side. In gateway side we're tracking your received payments then notify to you.
